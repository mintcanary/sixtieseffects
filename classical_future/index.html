Classical future

Next year will see the release of a second branch of sixties effects. These units will be built specifically for the use of classical instruments such as violins, cello’s, clarinet’s etc. Components will be modified to suit the needs of each individual instrument frequency range and each unit will have its own pick up and movable eq interface for ease of use.

The first of these will be an analog delay. Get in touch for more info.
