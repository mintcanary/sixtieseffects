Interdimensional Time Travelling Effects

Sixties effects is a small independent company producing effects units using technology and components from both the past and the future. The price of vintage “boutique” pedals can be extortionate and more often than not these units can be unreliable due to their age. Most if not all of the parts used in effects pedals during the 60’s and 70’s can still be found today. When old ideas and technology are mixed with new the results are an effect pedal with the sound and hand built quality of the past, and the reliability of a present day effects unit. If you have any problems with any sixties effect you can send it back to us at any time, an option that can’t be redeemed with an obsolete company from 1965. Every pedal made is also available in a version specifically for use with bass for the same price.
